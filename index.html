About SNIA

The SLASSCOM National Ingenuity Awards is Sri Lanka’s leading platform recognizing technological innovation across corporates, startups, universities, and schools.

Now in its 8th edition, SNIA highlights groundbreaking solutions that demonstrate originality, commercial potential, and measurable societal impact.

Mission

To inspire innovation, build intellectual property, and support Sri Lanka’s ambition of becoming a US$5 billion knowledge economy by 2030.

Eligibility

Open to registered companies in Sri Lanka, ICT startups registered after 1 Jan 2022, University students, and School students.

Judging Criteria

Submissions are rigorously evaluated based on Originality, Scalability, Practicality, and Impact.

Evaluation Process

A structured journey: Stage 1 – Initial Screening, Stage 2 – Provincial Evaluation, Stage 3 – National Finals.

Our Focus

What SNIA Does

Driving Sri Lanka’s transition from service-based to product innovation.

1. Discover & Recognise

Identify innovations from schools, universities, startups, and enterprises across Sri Lanka.

2. Evaluate & Validate

Entries are assessed through a multi-stage process:
Initial screening → Provincial review → National finals

3. Celebrate & Amplify

Winners are celebrated at a national gala at Shangri-La Colombo.

4. Inspire the Next Wave

Encouraging the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

5. Build IP Ownership

Promoting Sri Lanka’s transition from service-based to product innovation.

6. Attract Investment

Highlighting high-potential innovations to investors and accelerating startup growth.
